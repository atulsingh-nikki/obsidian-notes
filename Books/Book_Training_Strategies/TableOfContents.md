## About This Book

This book provides a comprehensive guide to training strategies for computer vision models. From fundamental optimization techniques to cutting-edge self-supervised learning methods, we explore the theoretical foundations and practical implementations that enable modern computer vision systems to achieve state-of-the-art performance.

Each chapter includes:
- Theoretical foundations
- Mathematical formulations
- Practical implementation guidance
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Best practices and common pitfalls
